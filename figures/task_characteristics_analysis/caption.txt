Figure 3: Taskmaster Task Characteristics Analysis. (A) Modern grouped bar chart showing the distribution of Judgment Types (Objective, Subjective) across each Activity Type (Creative, Mental, Physical, Social). Analysis of all 917 tasks reveals that Physical tasks (48.1%) and Objective evaluation (55.9%) are most common, while Creative tasks (43.5%) are often evaluated Subjectively (41.5%). Tasks can be evaluated using both objective and subjective criteria simultaneously. (B) Stacked bar chart showing the distribution of task activity types across series 1-18, revealing how task design has evolved over time.
