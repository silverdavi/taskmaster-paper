Figure 5: Contestant Performance Archetypes Across 18 Series. Each subplot represents one series, with the five contestants positioned according to their performance archetype: Steady Performer (top-left), Late Bloomer (top-right), Early Star (bottom-left), Chaotic Wildcard (bottom-right), and Consistent Middle (center). Series winners are highlighted with gold circles. The analysis reveals that each series contains exactly one contestant of each archetype type, suggesting a balance of different performance patterns may contribute to the show's entertainment value. These archetypes were determined by analyzing 15 performance metrics, including score progression, rank changes, and performance consistency across tasks.
